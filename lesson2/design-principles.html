Repetition

Cool Math Games

https://www.coolmathgames.com/

The cool math games site shows the pattern of repetition to be through colors of the names, the boarders on the photos, and the fonts of the titles and discriptions of each game.

Visual Alignment

Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints

https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/?lang=eng

The Church of Jesus Christ website shows visual alignment to me because the photos all align with eachother. Also, the text is centered under every photo.

Visual Balance

Fed Ex

https://www.fedex.com/en-us/home.html

The FedEx site shows visual balance to me because although the items on the page are not all the same size, they balance eachother out making the page very visualy appealing.
