Places to Stay

Super 8 Motel

Super 8 motel is one of the local places to stay in Grangeville. It is right off of the highway and next to Subway. There is a pool, hot tub, cable tv, and even computers you can use. There are conference rooms available if you need them as well.

If you are not staying at the Super 8 but still wish to use the pool you are able to with an admittence fee of $5.

Address: 801 W South 1st St, Grangeville, ID 83530

Things to Do

Public Pool

Our public pool is only open in the summer. They have two slides and a diving board! Their hours are Monday-Thursday: 7 AM-8:30 PM; Friday: 7-8 AM, 9:30 AM-4:40 PM, and 6-7:30 PM; Saturday-Sunday: 1-5 PM

Address: 100-198 Park St, Grangeville, ID 83530

Columbian Mammoth Exhibit

Grangeville found Mammoth bones in Tolo Lake! Find the exhibit on Highway 95 at Pine Street in the Soltman Park behind our Grangeville Chamber of Commerce Visitors Center. Grangeville's mammoth replica, 14 feet height and 17 feet long from tusk to tail was a five-year project to partially recreate the creature, based on mammoth bones discovered at nearby Tolo Lake. ( 5 miles west of Grangeville.)
